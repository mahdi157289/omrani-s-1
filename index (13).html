Our Story

A Legacy of Sweetness

For over two centuries, our family has preserved the art of traditional pastry making. Each recipe is a carefully guarded secret, passed down from master to apprentice.

Our museum-shop celebrates this heritage, presenting each pastry as the work of art it truly is. We source only the finest ingredients from their places of origin.
